ABOUT US

Welcome to Square eyes, where creativity meets opportunity. We're more than just a streaming platform; we're a community of passionate filmmakers, storytellers, and artists dedicated to celebrating the world of independent cinema.

OUR MISSION

At Square eyes, our mission is simple yet profound: to empower and uplift small filmmakers from around the globe. We believe that every voice deserves to be heard, and every story deserves to be seen. We're committed to providing a platform where emerging talents can showcase their unique visions and connect with a global audience. Why We Exist The world of filmmaking is vast and diverse, filled with incredible stories waiting to be told. Unfortunately, many brilliant voices often struggle to find their place in an industry dominated by big studios and established names. We recognized this gap and decided to bridge it. We exist to give talented filmmakers, regardless of their background or resources, the opportunity to shine.
